Our Story

Research-Based Development

Gaazna is a research-based product developed through extensive analysis of over 250 samples from Uttarakhand, revealing exceptional curcumin content ranging from 7% to 11%.

Scientific Validation

Our samples were analyzed at CSIR-IHBT Palampur, Government of India Lab, and compared with control varieties from the Indian Institute of Spice Research (IISR-ICAR).

Community Engagement

Commercial farming progresses with active farmer participation, especially women, in the village Dhauntri (Gaazna Patti), supporting local communities and sustainable practices.
